About Nagpur

Nagpur the second capital of Maharashtra, India, is uniquely situated in the geographical centre of India. The zero Milestone of India is in this city. It enjoys excellent connectivity to rest of the country through three National Highways as well as by rail and air connections to major cities of the country. It has generally a dry tropical weather. The monsoon starts in the month of June and extends upto September. In July, due to rainy season, the temperature is around 30-35° C. Rich in agriculture and horticulture produce, Nagpur is famous for its oranges and is also known as 'Orange City'. Huge coal and mineral deposits exist in this region along with some of the best infrastructure in the form of educational institutions, health facilities and a five star Industrial area at Butibori, the largest in Asia. Nagpur, The second greenest city in India has a plethora of tourists spots for the wildlife enthusiastic like the Dr Salim Ali National Bird Sanctuary at Nawegaon, Tiger Parks at Pench and Tadoba and picturesque treks to the Satpuda mountains. There are three huge aqua parks. Nagpur also boasts of a cosmopolitan populace, luxurious hotels & restaurants. As the nerve centre of Vidarbha, Nagpur pulsates with a unique blend of new and old tradition and heritage. The recent advancement in Nagpur is a MIHAN, a Multi-modal International Hub Airport at Nagpur, the work for which has just begin, which is attracting many business persons across the World, as it has acquired a status of "First City" in India in infrastructure development.

Getting from the Airport to Conference Venue

From Nagpur Airport: Please take a taxi to the GHRCE Autonomous campus. It may take 30 minutes from Airport, Nagpur.

Getting from the Railway Station to Conference Venue

The nearest (13 Km.) railway station is Nagpur, Maharashtra. You may take an Auto from Nagpur Railway Station. You may also hire a taxi from Nagpur railway station to the GHRCE campus.

Getting from the Bus to Conference Venue

This GHRCE, Nagpur campus is located near MIDC Hingna. GHRCE campus is about 11 KM. from Nagpur Bus Stand. You can city bus to reach GHRCE from main bus stand.

Local Weather & Time

Time zone: IST (Indian Standard Time) which is UTC +5:30. In November, Minimum temperature would be approximately 21°C to 25°C . It is advisable to you to bring the appropriate winter clothes.
