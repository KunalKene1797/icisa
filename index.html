International Conference on Emerging Trends in Engineering & Technology on Information Security and Analytics

Department of Computer Science & Engineering | G. H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur, India

01-02 June 2018

Previous Next

The conference will bring together experts from the information security and information Analytics to discuss the timely issue of information security and Analytics. This will provide a forum for sharing insights, experiences and interaction on various aspects of evolving technologies and patterns related to Computer Science. Authors are solicited to contribute to the conference by submitting articles that illustrate research results, projects, surveying works and industrial experiences that describe significant advances in the following areas, but are not limited to that.

Information Security
• Information Security & Privacy for Smart Devices , Mobile & IoT Security
• Mobile & IoT Security
• Wireless network privacy and anonymity
• Secure localization and location privacy
• Cellular network fraud and security
• Jamming attacks and defenses
• Security protocols
• Security in distributed systems
• Cloud Computing Security
• Secured Data Mining
• Ethical & legal implications of security & privacy
• Peer to peer security
• Security verification & validation
• Digital forensics and hacking techniques.

Analytics
• Analytics, Intelligence & Response
• Data Analytics
• Big Data
• High Performance and Computing
• Algorithms
• Social networks & Analytics
• Machine learning
